How it works

Five steps. Roughly 30 days.

The process is well-defined — most of it is paperwork we do for you. Your only job is deciding to go solar.

01

Free site survey

Our engineer visits, measures usable rooftop area, checks shading and load capacity. Same week.

02

Custom design + quote

You receive a sized BOQ, generation forecast, total cost, subsidy estimate and ROI in writing.

03

Subsidy & loan filing

We register you on the PM Surya Ghar portal, file with DISCOM and arrange financing if needed.

04

Install & commissioning

2–4 days of on-site work. Panels, inverter, structure, wiring, safety. Net meter installed.

05

Generate & get paid

Subsidy lands in your bank in 30 days. Your meter runs backwards. We monitor for 5 years.
